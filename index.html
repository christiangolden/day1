Game Rules

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic hand game played between two players.

How to Play:

  • Rock (✊) beats Scissors (✌️)
  • Scissors (✌️) beats Paper (✋)
  • Paper (✋) beats Rock (✊)

Controls:

  • Click the buttons or use keyboard shortcuts:
  • 'R' for Rock
  • 'P' for Paper
  • 'S' for Scissors

The game is played in best-of-three rounds. The player with the most wins after three rounds wins the game!
